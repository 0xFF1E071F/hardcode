  Binary Fight  :-: Ein Spiel in 3kb

  Code          :-: TGGC

  Packer        :-: Crinkler 0.4a (http://www.crinkler.net)


	
  Aim with the mouse and fire with one of the three buttons for
  left, middle and right cannon or use "A" "S" "D". Your score a
  point for every destroyed red attacker. Your ammunition is
  refilled if you scored 8 points. If an attacker hits the ground,
  one point gets substracted.
