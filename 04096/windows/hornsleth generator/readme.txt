Requirements
============
DirectX Runtime June 2010 installed, DirectX 11 graphics card


Info
====
Procedural Image generator (roughly so far) imitating the style of Kristian Hornsleth. 
Just give it a seed and a title as arguments, and Generate.exe will create you a unique image.
