Compatibility
=============
OpenGL, DX10 level graphics card. That is: Radeon HD 2x00+ or Geforce 8x00+. 
Tested working on HD3870/HD5770/8600GT/GTX260, latest drivers.

Info
====
Inspired by "Construction of the Tower of Babel" by Hendrick van Cleve III (1525-1589 Belgian) 

Raymarched distance functions.
Ambient occlusion, reflections and softshadows.
Two pass paint filter with supersampling, tone mapping etc.
Around 12kb glsl source before obfuscation and compression
