Guide the marble ball to the rotating star formed exit.
The skulls are shooting lasers, lasers are bad for you!
Push balls to block lasers.
Rotate map with the "a" and "d" keys or "q" and "e" for negative rotation.
Steer with arrow keys.

You got a life force of 1000, use it wisely!
It is possible (although very hard) to finish all five levels without spending life force.
Can you do it?
