- the animation is synced to the music

Troubleshooting:
If the intro does not start properly, then in most
cases, there are two reasons for this:

- DirectX
You need the DirectX Version 9c April 2005. The
demo only runs with this version of DirectX. You
can download an appropriate version from our
website: http://www.bitpopler.de/directx9_25.exe

- 3D Texture
This Demo uses a 3d marble texture. This is not
supported by older graphics cards.

- Anything else
You may send us an email to tell us what happens
when you run our demo. Perhaps we are able to fix
it...

We prepare (or already released) a video capture
and a soundtrack of Rodin on our website. So
everyone should be able to have a look at it.
