                     Artificial incident started as a rendertest for

                     transformfeedback and 3d volumetexture rendering.


                     And yes it's just another particle demo and runs only

                     on the latest gfx cards.. It just features surface

                     lighting on the basis of the particles itself. 
 

                     Too tired at the moment.. If you wanna try to code TF 
 
                     or 3d texture blits using geometryshaders 

                     check the source.. 
                     

                     I'm just happy to release something here at evoke...
