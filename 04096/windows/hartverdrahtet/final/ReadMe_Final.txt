 A 2012 interpretation of "Hardwired" in 4096 bytes. 
                    Celebrating more than 20 years of a legendary classic.

 Final version wich fixes the "mousecursor issue" wich was really annoying
 during the compo @Revision and also seems to make trouble on a couple of
 ATI cards. Thx to Seven/Fulcrum for pointing me in the right direction, 
 and of course las/Mercury for providing a detailed solution on poeut.net:
 http://www.pouet.net/topic.php?which=8720&page=1

 At least it does work on all of my test machines correctly now.

 Other than that I have added a couple of "HQ Super Sampling" versions to
 the archive wich render everything with 2x, 4x and 8xFSSS resulting in
 an absolute awesome visual quality. The downside of course is you 
 probably have to buy a new GPU :)
