Requirements
============
DirectX 11 graphics card (probably >1gb - large ray buffers)
DirectX Runtime June 2010 installed - OR d3dcompiler_47.dll (faster, included in win 8.1)


Info
====
Pathtracing with dof, softshadows, fuzzy reflections and diffuse interreflections.
Basicly a new scene for the system also used for Revision 2015 picture.
