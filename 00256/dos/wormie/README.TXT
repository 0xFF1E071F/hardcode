±°  How to play:

  As this is a 2-player game you have to be two persons.
One controlling the green worm by using A(left), W(up), S(down) and D(right),
the other controls the red worm by using the cursor/arrow-keys.
A player looses a point by going into himself, the other worm or the blue wall,
Therefore a player must avoid that and try to capture/kill his opponent.
To win a player must have a lead in 10 points over the other player.
The game can be cancelled at any time by pressing ESC.

±°  Features:

  This action-packed 2-player Worm/Tron Game boasts the following features:

þ (Intelligent) Multi-colored 320x200x256 VGA Graphics

þ Intelligent Keyboard-Handler including:
   þ Ignoring of other keys than 'awsd', arrows and ESC, not ending the game

þ Intelligent Score-System
   þ Left score in green for green worm and red score in the right for red worm
