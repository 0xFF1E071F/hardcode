³ Tipz And Trickz ³
ÀÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÙ

The levels in the game are quite hard. They are huge and full of rocks so some
good advices might be welcomed...

ş The screen borders won't hold you, so if you walk through the right border
  you will find yourself from the left side of screen but you are a one line
  lower. But if you walk trough the upper border you will not appear on the
  bottom of the screen - in fact your guy will be somewhere in the depths of
  the video memory, but you are still able to walk past the rocks on the top
  line of screen. If you hold down the 'up' key for very very long time you
  will finally appear in the bottom of the level.
ş The borders won't hold rocks and gems either so be careful!
ş Just remember that rocks falls down. Sounds stupid, huh :-). But the point
  is that the falling rocks often blocks your way down. So good method to
  solve the level is to find a good path to the bottom of the level and start
  collecting there. A very cool way to use this method is to completely clear
  a few bottom lines from sand and then continue clearing just by holding down
  the 'left' key. When the left border is reached you appear on the right side
  but a one line upper.
ş Avoid dropping gems under the bottom line of the screen. They will not
  disappear - you still have to collect them to complete the level. Finding
  them may be nasty. Check out the next tip...
ş Every rock or gem dropped on the line 56 (which is six lines under the
  bottom of the screen) decreases the number of the remaining gems by one.
ş Try to figure out the behavior of 'any keys'. They can help you out from
  bad situations
