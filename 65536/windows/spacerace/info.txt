spacerace
a 64 kb game by jsmith [NML]

ingame controls:

UP ARROW - accelerate
DOWN ARROW - brake
LEFT ARROW - turn left
RIGHT ARROW - turn right

SPACEBAR - press and hold for nitro boost (works for 15 seconds)

1 - default camera
2 \
3 -- other cameras
4 /
5 - bumper camera

P - take a screenshot

ESCAPE - ingame menu


p.s.: spacerace level editor requires microsoft .net framework 3.5
