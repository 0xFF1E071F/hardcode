
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  Nebula by SQNY
  A 64k Intro
  Released at Assembly 2008
  
  Visuals: kurli
  Music: orlando boom

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Uses ProTrekkr by hitchhikr/Rebels for music ... http://pagesperso-orange.fr/franck.charlet/protrekkr.html
  and kkrunchy by ryg/Farbrausch for packing ... http://www.farbrausch.de/~fg/kkrunchy/
  Thanks to rale for debugging help.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  
  Requires hardware that supports Framebuffer Objects (GL_EXT_framebuffer_object) and OpenGL Shading 
  Language (GLSL). Any relatively new (SM3.0++) Geforce or Radeon should do.
