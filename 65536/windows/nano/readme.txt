"Compo filler #1: NANO" 64kb intro by the SandS (www.thesands.ru)
4 days prod for Chaos Constructions 2010 demoparty (St. Peterburg, Russia).

Used:
Plasticator demotool (www.demoscene.ru/plasticator) by f0x, TMK, BiTL and others.
4kLang sound engine and synth (4klang.untergrund.net) by Alcatraz.
MuCruncher packer by Epilogue.
Renoise Tracker music studio (www.renoise.com).

Working with sources:
Download 4kLang, unpack to VST instruments folder.
Run Renoise, load nano-music.xrns then press "Load patch" and choose nano-music.4kp. Render to WAV (if you have registered version of Renoise) and convert it to ogg.
Download Plasticator, replace micra.wbmp from this archive (it's the font).
Unpack, go to Release folder and run exe file.
In top menu choose "File", then "Load pack". Point to nano.psc (Plasticator project).
In "Tracker/timeline" window press "..." button and select nano-music.ogg (rendered with Renoise).
Press "play with music". Enjoy.

To compile exe choose "Make *.h" from "File" menu.

Video version can be found on Manwe's channel (www.youtube.com/Matchugovsky).
