Volcano, 37920Byte intro


History:
Thursday evening I heard the news of a scene party, and started coding my first 64k intro of my life. Finished on the party just before the compo started. Thanks a lot to all of you who, helped me!

Features:
- Uses Direct3D
- Shadows with multiple light sources
- Lighting on flat surfaces
- Volume lights
- Blurring the screen
- all scenes syncronized to the music

Hardware needed:
- Really fast video card (eg. GeForce3, or newest raedon)
- Support for stencil buffer
- Support for rendering into texture
