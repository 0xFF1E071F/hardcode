Weird Magic was founded somewhere in the dark ages, back in the old
days on MSX-2. Since 1991 'active' on PC, and since 1994 'active' in
the demo scene. Until 1994 I (Mr.Logic) was alone, and did not much
more than calling boards and be bored by it, a total lack of inspiration.
But then, on a bright day, MiG announced that he was searching for a
demo-group to join, because Bananacrap! Inc. died. I saw a great
oppertunity, and grabbed it with both hands. I called MiG and so it
happened that MiG became a member. A couple of friends I knew were
kind of artistic too, but not really on PC, or on any computer at all.
I decided that they could join too. Later on, The RamCrammer (TRC)
from Bananacrap! Inc. was back again, he was responsible for the death
of Bananacrap! Inc. but he got kinda homesick to the coding of demo
stuff :)  so he decided to join Weird Magic. We're all very happy with
it though! The next school year, I met 2 great guys who were kinda
freaky with PC's too. First Science joined as a coder, I was very happy
with it, 'cause I don't really have the time to do all the stuff alone.
Especially 'cause I was coding a music-system, which I haven't finished
even today! and 'cause TRC was/is coding special projects which involve
a great deal of science and stuff. Great for use in demo's or scene utils.
So Science was the first in Weird Magic who actually begon with vga
routines, and we were all happy again :)
Later Face joined as a graphician, he made a very cool logo for us,
which we will use forever :)
Oh yeah....And a lame dude called Guybrush payed me 100 bucks to join us
so he did ;). Someday there was a success meeting, which sucked in some ways,
but on the way back home, Nothingface wanted to join us, and after a long
discussion (0.1623 seconds) we agreed :))
Later Guybrush changed his handle to Seppe (wich even sucked more !)
