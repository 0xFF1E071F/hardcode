Rise of the Cubes

A RTS game in less than 96k.

How to play

Short version: left click for selecting, and right click for the default action.

Gathering resources: Left click on a worker, and right click on a crystal.
Attack: Left click on your unit, and right click on the unit or building to attack.
Build: Select a worker, press 'b' to select 'build' and then press the shortcut of the building you want to build
Multiple selection: Hold Ctrl while selecting units
Create group: Ctrl+num
Select group: num
