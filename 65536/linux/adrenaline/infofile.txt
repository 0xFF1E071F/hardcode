Adrenaline by PHn.

Released at the Mekka & Symposium 2001. 64kB intro / Linux.

Requires glibc2.1, XFree86 4.x, libSDL 1.2.x, hardware accelerated 
OpenGL-libraries (libGL.so) and OSS. Tested with G400, TNT2 and Geforce2.

- 32bpp.
- no ESD or ALSA, just OSS.
- 640x480.

Final version soon (Linux- and Bill32).
