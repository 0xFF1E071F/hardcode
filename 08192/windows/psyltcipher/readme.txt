Credits
=======
Music: Syltefar
Code: Psycho (psycho@loonies.dk)
Rocket scripting: Hornet (hornet@loopit.dk)

Crinkler used for compression, Clinkster(dual thread version) for music and Rocket as sync tool.


Compatibility
=============
DirectX 11 graphics card. 
We have had some problems with recent nvidia drivers crashing.

Expect a quite long music calulation time at start, like 30s.
